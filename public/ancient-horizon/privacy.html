1. Summary

Ancient Horizon is a single-player idle RPG with optional online features (leaderboards, chat, guilds, mail, and in-app purchases). We collect the minimum data needed to run those features. We do not sell your data, we do not run behavioral ad tracking, and we do not collect your real name, email (unless you choose to link an account), location, contacts, or device advertising identifiers.

2. Data we collect

Data Why we collect it Retention

We do not collect: your real name, plaintext email, precise or coarse location, device advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID), contacts, photos, microphone/camera data, or cross-app behavioral tracking data.

3. How we use data

We use the data above only to: run and save your game; provide optional online features (chat, leaderboards, guilds, mail); process and verify in-app purchases; detect cheating and abuse; respond to support requests; and comply with legal obligations. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Legal bases (GDPR, where applicable)

  • Performance of a contract — running the Game and the features you use.
  • Legitimate interests — fraud/cheat prevention, security, and service integrity.
  • Legal obligation — retaining purchase records for tax/accounting.
  • Consent — any optional feature you opt into (e.g., linking an account). You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. Who we share data with (processors)

We share data only with service providers that help us run the Game, under contract:

  • [BACKEND PROVIDER — e.g. Supabase] — hosts the backend database, authentication, and real-time services. Player data is isolated per account by row-level security.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handle account sign-in (if you link one) and all payments. We never receive or store your full payment details. Purchase receipts are validated server-to-server.
  • Apple App Attest / Google Play Integrity — device-integrity attestation used for anti-cheat. These return an attestation result; they are not used to track you.

We do not sell personal information or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

6. International transfers

Our backend is hosted in your region. If you access the Game from another region, your data may be processed in your region under appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses where required).

7. Security

Saves are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and integrity-protected (HMAC-SHA256). All network communication uses TLS 1.3. We minimize the personal data we collect and apply access controls on the backend. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable, industry-standard measures to protect your data.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and others), you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you;
  • Delete your account and associated data — in-Game via Settings → Account → Delete Account, which anonymizes your data within 30 days;
  • Correct inaccurate data (e.g., change your display name);
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent for any optional feature.

To exercise any right, use the in-Game deletion control or contact hello@ancienthorizon.game. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. California residents: we do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under the CPRA.

The Game offers optional in-app purchases of premium currency ("Gems") and contains randomized reward mechanics ("gacha"). Purchases are processed by Apple/Google. The odds for randomized rewards are disclosed in-game and in our Terms of Service. See the Terms of Service for full details.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in-Game or via the store listing, and the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued play after an update means you accept the revised policy.
