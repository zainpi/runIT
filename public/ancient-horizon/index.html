What's inside

Easy to start, hard to put down

A full idle RPG that runs while you live your life — then rewards every time you check back in.

Auto-battle, always on

Tap to fight or let auto-battle carry the party through a hand-crafted world of original creatures. Offline gains are real progress — never a consolation prize.

Fair, exciting gacha

Summon weapons, armor and abilities across seven rarities. Every banner shows its real odds and a visible pity counter that guarantees a top-tier reward.

Deep progression

An upgrade shop, a tabbed passive skill tree, gear stars, armor refinement, a six-slot ability bar, and a class that fits your playstyle.

Prestige & push further

Reset for compounding relic bonuses and blast back through content far faster than before. Every run feels at least three times quicker.

Dungeons & world bosses

Spend keys on dungeon runs and take down elites, commanders and world bosses across an ever-expanding ten-world map.

Play together

Join a guild, climb weekly leaderboards and chat with other players — all server-validated, so the rankings are always honest.

The fair-play promise

RNG that actually feels fair

Bad gacha is the number-one complaint in this genre, so we built ours the opposite way: exciting, transparent and never predatory.

  • ✓Hard pity is guaranteed and visible. A top-rarity reward by pull 100, with the counter always on screen.
  • ✓Free players clear everything. Premium currency is also earnable — nothing mandatory is locked behind a wallet.
